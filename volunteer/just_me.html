Just Me

You can sign up to volunteer immediately at these fine organizations.

St. Vincent de Paul Dining Hall

Sponsored by Catholic Community Services, this industrially designed kitchen prepares hot, fresh, and nutritious meals that are both served in their dining area to the needy and trucked to multiple shelters and resource centers in the greater Salt Lake area. You can sign up to prepare food or serve food.

  • Serve people experiencing homelessness and multiple shelters in the area
  • Prepare meals in their kitchen or serve meals
  • 437 W 200 S Salt Lake City, UT 84101
Volunteer

Pamela Atkinson Resource Center

This resource center for people experiencing homelessness provides hot meals, counseling services, and a safe place to sleep. It's sponsored by The Road Home, and they have a regular need for volunteers.

  • Serve people experiencing homelessness
  • Serve hot meals for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, or organize in-kind donations
  • 3380 S 1000 W, South Salt Lake, UT 84119
Volunteer

Recovery on Redwood

This men's detoxification center provides counseling and hot fresh meals, cooked on the premises. The goal is to assist those seeking detox and withdrawal management back on their feet.

  • Serve men utilizing the provided detox and counseling services
  • Serve lunch or dinner
  • 1875 S Redwood Rd, Salt Lake City, UT 84104
